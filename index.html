About me

I am a frontend developer from Niš. I was born in Kruševac, a smaller but very beautiful city near Niš. There I went to Grammar School, and then I moved to Niš and finished Faculty of Science and Mathematics, Department of Computer Science. I have one year of professional experience providing front-end development, producing high-quality responsive websites and exceptional user experience. The work I provide is of the highest quality, fully responsive, and tested in a wide range of devices. Often front-end development of a website is overlooked, but being the part of your project that combines design, back-end development and the layer that your users interact with, from my experience, it is the most important to get right. About myself, I can also say that I'm very good with organization, task handling, curious and fast learner, extremely motivated to constantly develop my skills and grow professionally. I love learning new things, implementing new technologies and aware of how much I still need to learn so I can upgrade my knowledge and skills.
